## About the Project
GOAT of Government: A Very Hooficial Business is a small highscore game, where the player takes the role of an important leader of a country, and, in order to stay in office, they need to influence the news about them. This is done by either approving the documents the player is handed, or by feeding them to their trusty goat. But they need to be careful which news they feed to the goat, since it only has limited hunger.

## Key Features
<ul>
    <li>ranged and melee enemies</li>
    <li>defeat enemies by cutting their strings</li>
</ul>
