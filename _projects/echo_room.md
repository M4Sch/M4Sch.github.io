## About the Project
Echo Room is a story driven puzzle game, made in collaboration with students from RVE and MMA.

## Key Features
<ul>
    <li>interaction system</li>
    <li>mirror for teleporting between rooms</li>
</ul>

## Development Process
Echo Room was developed in the 3rd semester of the master's studies of the cohort 2024.



## Technologies Used
<ul>
    <li>Unreal Engine</li>
    <li>C++</li>
    <li>Blueprints</li>
</ul>
